Romantic and historic Casa Cody Inn is the oldest operating hotel in Palm Springs. Designated as a Class I Historic Site, it was founded in the 1920's by the beautiful Hollywood pioneer, Harriet Cody, cousin to the legendary Buffalo Bill. Casa Cody is nestled against the spectacular San Jacinto mountains in the heart of Palm Springs Village.
